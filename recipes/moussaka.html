Description

A Mediterranean dish made with layers of roasted eggplant, spiced meat sauce, and creamy béchamel, similar to lasagna but with a unique flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1 lb (450g) ground lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz / 400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Basic Steps:
    1. Roast eggplant slices until tender.
    2. Prepare the meat sauce by browning the lamb or beef with onions, garlic, and spices, then simmering with tomatoes.
    3. Make the béchamel sauce by whisking butter, flour, and milk until thickened, then stirring in egg yolks and Parmesan.
    4. Layer roasted eggplant, meat sauce, and béchamel in a baking dish.
    5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes until golden brown.
