Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ziti or penne pasta
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or sausage
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Basic Steps:
    1. Cook pasta until al dente
    2. Brown the meat with onions and garlic, then simmer with marinara sauce and seasonings.
    3. Layer the cooked pasta, ricotta cheese, and meat sauce in a baking dish, topping with mozzarella and Parmesan.
    4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
